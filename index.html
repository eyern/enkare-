We offer the following services at Enkare Drilling Ltd


Hydro Geological Survey

Enkare Drilling Ltd perfoms geological assessments that focuses on groundwater resources, including their location, depth, quality, and potential for sustainable use.

Drilling & Casing Installation.

Installing drilling casing as the hole is drilled helps to stabilize loose, or unpredictable formations, and shore up the hole.

Flushing

Cleaning a borehole by removing debris, cuttings, and drilling fluids to improve water flow and prevent contamination.

Test Pumping

Enkare Drilling Ltd a crucial assesses the yield and sustainability of a new or existing borehole, helping to determine the optimal pumping rate and assess the impact on surrounding groundwater resources.

Pump Installation

It includes assessing the borehole, selecting the appropriate pump, and ensuring proper installation and maintenance.

Borehole Equipping

Installing necessary equipments, like pumps and accessories, within a drilled borehole to create a functional water supply system.

Frequently Asked Questions

As all borehole projects are bespoke we do not have a 'set' price. Please contact us through 0723-855-827 for a free quotation..

The time it takes to drill a borehole depends on several factors: The depth of the borehole, the type of ground, and the availability of equipment and personnel.

Consider your water needs: If you rely on a well for your primary water supply, a borehole can provide a more reliable and sustainable source of water.

A good constructed Borehole can last for generations to generations although some boreholes which require rehabilitation after some years to ensure that they are in good working order. In Kenya we have successfully drilled and rehabilitated many Boreholes for commercial and domestic use which are still up and running properly.

After drilling the borehole and doing test pumping, ABEco will take a water sample to the government laboratory for testing. The test results in a water analysis report that indicates whether or not the water is safe for domestic/industrial consumption.
